About BookScan App

What is BookScan App?

BookScan App is a powerful tool that uses computer vision and optical character recognition (OCR) to identify books from photos of bookshelves. Simply upload a photo of your bookshelf, and our application will detect individual books and extract text from their spines.

How It Works

Step 1: Upload

Take a photo of your bookshelf and upload it to our application.

Step 2: Process

Our algorithm detects individual books and processes each spine.

Step 3: Results

View a list of detected books with extracted text from each spine.

Technology

BookScan App uses several advanced technologies:

  • Computer Vision (OpenCV): For image processing and book detection
  • Optical Character Recognition (Tesseract): For extracting text from book spines
  • Flask: A lightweight Python web framework for the backend
  • Modern Web Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript for the user interface

Tips for Best Results

  • Take photos in good lighting conditions
  • Ensure book spines are clearly visible
  • Avoid glare on book covers
  • Position camera parallel to the bookshelf
  • Make sure text on book spines is readable
